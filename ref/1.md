### Actions to Improve or Create Your Trading Strategy

**1. Build a "Personal Investment Framework" Folder for AI Analysis**
You do not need to be a software engineer to build an automated research system. You can create a local folder (a "wing folder") containing Markdown files that detail your core investment strategies, rules, and philosophies. Point an AI tool (like Claude) to this directory so it learns your specific "world model." You can then simply prompt the AI to research a company, and it will automatically analyze the target and generate a customized report based strictly on your framework. 

**2. Filter "Noise" from "Structural Change"**
When analyzing the market, it is critical to separate daily noise (such as political tweets or short-term administrative orders) from genuine structural changes. For example, the realization that expensive, traditional defense systems cannot stop cheap drone swarms is a structural change, indicating a massive future demand for AI-driven defense upgrades. Base your long-term investments on these underlying structural shifts rather than reacting to daily noise.

**3. Use AI for Chart Reading and Dashboards**
AI models are highly adept at reading trading charts, often outperforming humans. You can feed trading curves and charts directly into the AI to have it automatically mark optimal buy and sell points. Furthermore, you can use the AI to turn your raw Markdown research notes into clean, dynamic visual dashboards to track your portfolio, eliminating the need for complex database software.

**4. Structure Your Portfolio Using the "5-Layer Model"**
Organize your watchlist and capital allocation systematically across five distinct layers to capture the structural changes brought by AI:
*   **Base Layer:** Energy (e.g., electricity, nuclear power, and essential metals).
*   **Compute Layer:** AI chips, data centers, and hardware infrastructure.
*   **Core Engine:** Foundational models.
*   **Application Layer:** Companies with proven, scalable revenue models successfully utilizing AI to deliver end-to-end services (such as advanced manufacturing, autonomous driving, and defense technology).
*   **Frontier Layer:** Space exploration, quantum computing, AI for science, and advanced robotics.

**5. Keep Your Long-Term Strategy Simple (and Prepare for 2027)**
Unless you are running a high-frequency quantitative fund, do not overcomplicate your strategy. The core of long-term investing remains simple: pick the right company, do not overpay for it, and exit at the right time. Additionally, anticipate a potential market shock or crash around 2027 resulting from massive AI-induced organizational restructuring and unemployment. Having capital ready to short the market or buy the dip during this period could be a highly profitable maneuver.
